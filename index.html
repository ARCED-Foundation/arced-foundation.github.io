Aureolin Research, Consultancy, and Expertise Development (ARCED) Foundation is a non-profit organization specializing in research, training, and monitoring. Established in 2013, it collaborates with national and international partners, earning recognition for its work.

ARCED conducts both quantitative and qualitative research, including real-time data collection and socio-economic assessments. It employs methodologies like RCTs, DiD, and statistical modeling using tools such as SPSS and STATA. The foundation has a team of in-house and external experts to ensure high-quality research and analysis.
